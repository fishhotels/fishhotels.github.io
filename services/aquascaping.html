Aquascaping

Designing healthy, natural aquatic environments to support safe ecosystems and pollution-free habitats.

Natural Habitats

We recreate natural underwater landscapes using eco-friendly materials that help fish feel safe and reduce stress.

  • Rock formations
  • Sheltering structures
  • Plant-safe substrate
  • Biologically stable zones

Live Aquatic Plants

Plants increase oxygen levels, improve water quality, and provide natural hiding spaces for fish.

  • Java Fern
  • Hornwort
  • Anubias
  • Water Wisteria

Sustainable Materials

FishDome prioritizes pollution-free, non-toxic materials that are safe for long-term aquatic use.

  • Certified-safe substrates
  • Non-polluting décor
  • Filtered stones & driftwood
  • Biodegradable components

Core Aquascaping Principles

Balance & Stability

All designs ensure water flow, oxygenation, and safe zones for fish to rest, hide, and feed.

Environmental Safety

We avoid any plastics, paints, or materials that could degrade and contribute to microplastic pollution.

Ecological Realism

Aquascapes mimic natural ecosystems, promoting healthier and more stable fish behavior.

Aquascaping in FishDome Systems

Stress Reduction

Fish retreat into natural plant and rock structures, reducing stress caused by light, noise, and pollutants.

Water Quality Improvements

Plants absorb nitrates and CO₂, supporting healthy dome environments without chemicals.

Habitat Restoration

Our aquascapes are built to mimic real ecosystems, helping reintroduce fish safely into restored waterways.
